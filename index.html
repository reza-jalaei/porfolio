This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use this site, how it is used, and your choices. This site loads several third‑party scripts in the “Tracking Snippets” section solely to improve performance, usability, and content quality. We do not sell visitor personal information.

Data we process

  • Interaction data (e.g., clicks, scrolls, navigation flows). Some tools may provide session replays to help diagnose UX issues.
  • Network and approximate location as derived from IP address (generally aggregated or truncated, depending on the provider).
  • Form content you submit (e.g., name, email, message) via the contact form is sent to our form processor for delivery.
Tracking Snippets used on this site
  • Cloudflare Beacon — site reliability and performance telemetry to keep the site fast and available. See Cloudflare Privacy.

  • Rybbit — Core Web Vitals and quality analytics to monitor page health. See Rybbit.
  • Umami — privacy‑friendly analytics used to count visits and pages without ad tracking cookies. See Umami Privacy.
  • FullStory — experience analytics/session replays to diagnose issues and improve usability. See FullStory Privacy.
  • RB2B — When you visit or log in to our website, cookies and similar technologies may be used by our online data partners or vendors to associate these activities with other personal information they or others have about you, including by association with your email. We (or service providers on our behalf) may then send communications and marketing to these email. You may opt out of receiving this advertising by visiting https://app.retention.com/optout.
Cookies, storage, and consent

Some providers use cookies or local storage for sampling, session continuity, or feature toggles. Where applicable, these scripts honor your consent preferences managed by your browser or cookie tools. You can block or clear cookies at any time in your browser settings.

Legal bases and data sharing

We process analytics data under legitimate interests (to maintain and improve the site) and/or consent where required by law. Service providers may act as processors on our behalf. We do not sell personal information.

Retention

We retain analytics data for the minimum period needed to measure trends and troubleshoot issues. Aggregated metrics may be kept longer for longitudinal analysis.

Your choices and opt‑out controls
  • Use browser features such as private windows, tracker‑blocking, and “Do Not Track.”
  • Manage cookies/storage in your browser or via your consent/cookie banner if present.
  • Provider pages: Cloudflare, Microsoft Clarity, FullStory, Umami, Rybbit, RB2B.
  • RB2B specific opt‑outs https://app.retention.com/optout and, if applicable, GDPR opt‑out at https://www.rb2b.com/rb2b-gdpr-opt-out.
